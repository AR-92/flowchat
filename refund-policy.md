
# Refund Policy for FlowChat

At FlowChat, we are committed to your satisfaction. If you're not completely satisfied with our AI chatbot service, we offer a **7-day refund policy**.

## 1. Eligibility for Refund
You are eligible for a refund if you request it within **7 days** of using the FlowChat service. Refund requests will only be considered if:

- You have not used the service beyond the 7-day period.
- You have experienced technical issues that prevent you from using the service.

## 2. How to Request a Refund
To request a refund, please follow these steps:

1. Email us at **support@flowchat.com** with your refund request.
2. Include your account details, the date you started using the service, and the reason for your refund request.

## 3. Refund Process
Once your refund request is received, we will review your case and process the refund within **5 business days**. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.

## 4. Exceptions
Refunds will **not** be provided in the following cases:

- The service has been used beyond the 7-day refund period.
- The issue with the service was caused by user error or misuse.
- The account holder has violated our Terms & Conditions.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We reserve the right to update or modify this Refund Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and your continued use of the service will be considered acceptance of the updated policy.
