The Challenge

Capital Partners, a mid-sized investment firm managing $2.8B in assets, was struggling with an extremely time-intensive client onboarding process that was limiting their growth potential. Their manual onboarding workflow required 72 hours per client, creating a significant bottleneck that prevented them from scaling their business effectively.

Key Pain Points

  • 72 hours required to onboard each new client
  • Only able to process 40 clients per year
  • 85% of onboarding time spent on document processing
  • Frequent compliance verification errors

Business Impact

  • Lost business opportunities due to slow onboarding
  • High operational costs ($450/client onboarding cost)
  • Compliance risks from manual verification
  • Limited capacity to serve high-net-worth clients

The firm was missing out on significant revenue opportunities because they couldn't onboard clients quickly enough to meet demand. They needed a solution that could automate the document-intensive portions of their onboarding process while maintaining strict compliance standards.

Our Solution

We implemented a comprehensive client onboarding automation solution that integrated with Capital Partners' document management system, compliance database, and CRM platform. The solution automated document collection, verification, and data entry while maintaining full audit trails for regulatory compliance.

Implementation Approach

1

System Integration (Weeks 1-2)

We integrated our automation platform with Capital Partners' document management system, compliance verification databases, and CRM. This allowed seamless data flow between systems and eliminated manual data entry.

2

AI Document Processing (Weeks 3-4)

We configured AI-powered document processing workflows to automatically extract, verify, and validate client information from various document types including tax returns, bank statements, and identification documents. The system learned to recognize document types and apply appropriate verification rules.

3

Testing & Compliance Validation (Weeks 5-6)

We conducted extensive testing with real client data (anonymized) to ensure accuracy and compliance. The compliance team validated that all regulatory requirements were met, and we implemented automated audit trail generation for every step of the process.

Technical Implementation

Core Technologies

  • Optical Character Recognition (OCR) engines
  • Machine learning document classification
  • API integrations with compliance databases

Key Features

  • Automated document collection and processing
  • Real-time compliance verification
  • Complete audit trail generation
