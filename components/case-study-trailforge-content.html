The Challenge

TrailForge, a leading luggage manufacturer with annual revenues of $85M, was experiencing significant financial losses due to poor inventory management. Their traditional forecasting methods were resulting in 35% inventory waste from overstocking slow-moving items and stockouts of popular products, directly impacting their bottom line and customer satisfaction.

Key Pain Points

  • 35% inventory waste from overstocking
  • 22% stockout rate for popular items
  • $735,000 annual loss from inventory mismanagement
  • Manual forecasting based on limited historical data

Business Impact

  • Cash flow tied up in excess inventory
  • Lost sales revenue from stockouts
  • Increased storage and handling costs
  • Customer dissatisfaction from delayed orders

The company was losing competitive advantage to more agile competitors who could respond quickly to market demands. They needed a solution that could predict demand patterns with greater accuracy while optimizing their entire supply chain.

Our Solution

We implemented an AI-driven inventory forecasting system that integrated with TrailForge's ERP, sales channels, and supplier networks. The solution used machine learning algorithms to analyze 3 years of historical sales data, seasonal trends, market conditions, and external factors to predict demand with unprecedented accuracy.

Implementation Approach

1

ERP Integration (Weeks 1-2)

We integrated our predictive analytics platform with TrailForge's ERP system to access real-time inventory levels, sales data, and procurement information. This enabled seamless data flow and eliminated manual reporting requirements.

2

Model Training (Weeks 3-4)

We trained machine learning models on 3 years of historical sales data, incorporating seasonal patterns, promotional campaigns, and external market factors. The models learned to identify demand patterns for different product categories and SKUs.

3

Supply Chain Integration (Weeks 5-6)

We connected the forecasting system to supplier networks and procurement workflows to automate reorder points and optimize order quantities. The system learned supplier lead times and automatically adjusted forecasts based on supply chain constraints.

Technical Implementation

Core Technologies

  • Machine learning demand prediction models
  • API integrations with ERP and supplier systems

Key Features
