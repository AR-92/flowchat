Pricing for FlowChat AI Chatbot

At FlowChat, we offer flexible pricing to suit your business needs. Below are the details of our pricing plans.

1. Base Bot

Price: $1,000 (one-time payment)

The Base Bot includes the core features of our AI chatbot, designed to handle customer inquiries, provide automated support, and enhance user engagement.

Features:

  • Natural language processing (NLP) support
  • 24/7 customer service automation
  • Customizable responses
  • Integration with major platforms (e.g., website, Facebook, WhatsApp)
  • Basic analytics and reporting

2. Add-Ons and Advanced Features

Advanced Analytics: $300 (one-time payment)

Gain deeper insights into your chatbot’s performance with advanced reporting, sentiment analysis, and detailed interaction logs.

Custom Integrations: Starting at $500

Need custom integrations with your existing software? We can help you integrate FlowChat with your CRM, ticketing system, or any other platform.

Priority Support: $200/month

Get priority customer support with faster response times, dedicated assistance, and troubleshooting.

Multilingual Support: $400 (one-time payment)

Add support for additional languages to help you engage customers from around the world.

3. Subscription Plans (Optional)

Basic Plan: $50/month

  • Access to the Base Bot
  • Up to 1,000 monthly interactions
  • Standard support

Pro Plan: $150/month

  • Access to the Base Bot + Advanced Features
  • Up to 10,000 monthly interactions
  • Priority support
  • Custom reporting

Enterprise Plan: Custom Pricing

  • Unlimited interactions
  • Custom integrations and features
  • Dedicated account manager and 24/7 support
  • Tailored solutions based on your needs

4. Payment Terms

  • All pricing is in USD.
  • Payments can be made via credit card, PayPal, or bank transfer.
  • One-time payments (Base Bot and Add-Ons) are non-refundable, except as outlined in our Refund Policy.
  • Monthly subscriptions are billed annually but can be canceled at any time.
