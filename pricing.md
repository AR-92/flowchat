
# Pricing for FlowChat AI Chatbot

At FlowChat, we offer flexible pricing to suit your business needs. Below are the details of our pricing plans.

## 1. Base Bot

**Price**: $1,000 (one-time payment)

The Base Bot includes the core features of our AI chatbot, designed to handle customer inquiries, provide automated support, and enhance user engagement.

### Features:
- Natural language processing (NLP) support
- 24/7 customer service automation
- Customizable responses
- Integration with major platforms (e.g., website, Facebook, WhatsApp)
- Basic analytics and reporting
